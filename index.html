Shruthi over years

CBIT's Cultural Triumph

Over the years, from 2017 to 2023, Shruthi, CBIT's annual cultural fest, transformed into a dynamic showcase of talent. Evolving from its inception, it became a platform for students to express their creativity through music, dance, and theater. With each passing year, Shruthi grew in popularity, contributing to the vibrant cultural fabric of CBIT and leaving a lasting legacy of artistic excellence.
